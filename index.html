The Dice Game 🎲

The rules are simple. The first player to reach 100 points wins. When you roll the dice the first time the points are added to your current score. You can decide to roll the dice again or hold your points and add them to your total score. Be careful because if you roll a 1, your current score will be lost and it will be your opponent's turn to play. Good luck!
